Kia ora, I'm Nick. I work in Wellington as an Investment Manager in MBIE's Science System Investment and Performance team, where I manage contestable research funding and contracts that support New Zealand's science system.

My background started in biochemistry, where I completed a PhD, before moving into research administration. I worked in pre-award research support at the University of Auckland, including Horizon Europe applications, and later in funding support roles at the Royal Society Te Apārangi across the Marsden Fund and Catalyst Fund.

Outside of work, I have a long-standing interest in science diplomacy, governance and board roles, and the wider research and innovation landscape across the Asia-Pacific and Eurasia. This site is where I write about that thinking, alongside notes on courses I'm taking, places I visit, and small projects I'm working on.

Everything here reflects my own views and is written in my own time. It doesn't represent MBIE or any organisation I'm involved with.
