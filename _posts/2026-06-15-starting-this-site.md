I've put this site together as a place to write things down. Mostly it will be notes on courses I'm doing, ideas about science policy and research investment, and the odd thought about planning, water, or Wellington.

The aim is to keep it simple. There's an About page, a running list of projects I'm working on, this blog, and a page for what I'm reading. No comments, no tracking, nothing clever. Just a place to put things in my own words, in my own time.

If you've found this through LinkedIn or somewhere similar, welcome. Everything here is personal and doesn't represent my employer or any organisation I'm involved with.
